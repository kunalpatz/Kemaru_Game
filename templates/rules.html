In this logic game, you have to find the missing numbers in areas made up of 1 to 5 boxes.

An area of a box necessarily contains the number 1, an area of two boxes contains

the numbers 1 and 2, an area of three boxes contains the numbers 1, 2 and 3, etc.

Two identical numbers cannot be placed side by side side by side, nor diagonally.
